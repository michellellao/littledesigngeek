Streamly - Android App

Android Mobile Design, Visual & Interaction Design, UX

Streamly is an Android app that works with Chromecast as a controller of what's playing on the big screen.
It's also a discovery tool for users to browse other recommended videos based on what they watched and what video streaming sites they use.

Streamly was conceived at a coffee shop.

I had my sketch book with me so I took notes and wireframed on it.

The team of 3 (front-end dev, back-end dev, and me) worked together to brainstorm features, and I turned them into crude wireframes on paper.

Then, I worked individually to create a higer fidelity wireframe, deatiling the user flow and interaction.

Here's a comp.

For next version, adding a chat or comment system could be useful.
Though that's still in "sketch" stage.
