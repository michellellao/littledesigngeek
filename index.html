About Michelle


How I Started

At age 5, I fell in love with making cool looking things, when my crab house water color won the local contest.

At age 14, I started doing art and design for web with an awesome developer; I've been doing fun projects ever since.

What I do

I design simple, thoughtful, and fun interactions. I love getting into the user's mind and designing something that is intuitive and beautiful.

I'm obsessed with games and gamification. I follow game industry news daily, and I make games as a hobby.

What Else?

When I’m not geeking out learning new design and tech stuff, I’m geeking out making or playing video games and music.

I play at the local Open Mic at Red Rock Cafe almost every Monday, and I'm known as the girl with the looper.
